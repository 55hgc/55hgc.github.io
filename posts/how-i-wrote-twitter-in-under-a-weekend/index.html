How I Wrote Twitter in Under a Weekend

September 12, 2023

Twitter

The Process

First, I opened ChatGPT and instructed it with the prompt Write "Twitter".

Next, I double clicked ChatGPT’s output to select it, and right clicked to copy it to my clipboard.

Next, I opened Neovim and used the command :"+y to paste from my clipboard.

Next, I used the command :w to finally write Twitter.
